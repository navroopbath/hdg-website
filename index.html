What services do you provide?

The Hard Drive Guys focus on data recovery. We recover information on PCs/Macs that have crashed, been attacked by viruses, accidental deletion, etc.

How does it work?

Contact us and fill out the information in the required fields. One of our team members will get back to you with a few hours to set up an appointment. Once the team member arrives at the location of your choosing, we'll recover your information. Or, if you are unable to meet, we can arrange a pick-up and drop-off. If for some reason a team member is unable to recover your data, you don't pay a penny.

What are your hours of operation?

Anytime, anywhere in the South Bay. Shoot us an email and we'll get back to you within a few hours. If you'd like, we can provide our recovery services within 24 hours of initial contact.
